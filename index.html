Interactive Nature Scene

Experience a living, breathing digital garden that responds to your every interaction

About This Project

This interactive nature scene combines the beauty of the natural world with creative coding. Using p5.js, we've created a dynamic environment that responds to user interaction, bringing together day and night cycles, seasonal changes, and wildlife in a harmonious digital ecosystem.

Day & Night Cycle

Watch as the sky transforms from day to night with a simple hover. Experience the magic of twinkling stars appearing in the night sky and moving flowers.

Seasonal Changes

Experience the magic of winter as you scroll down the page. Watch as the scene transforms with falling snow and a winter wonderland atmosphere.

Interactive Wildlife

Create your own butterfly garden by clicking on the flowers. Each butterfly has it's own unique flight pattern and colour.
